V1 update

I have created my first website about hunting in Washington State.
I have created 5 pages to include my cascading style sheet (css). The css is what makes my site look like the way it does, it compliments the html by giving it structure. There are navigational links on all pages so you can navigate everywhere you need to in my site. Included are also external hyperlinks that provide additional information on hunting in Washington State. Information is also available for the regulations in Washington for hunting and a little more on what type of hunting you can do.


V2 update

I have continued to add more to my website.
I have added a drop down menu that takes you to an external website that will lead you to purchasing certain licenses or permits. I have added mobile and tablet conversion
for more of a diverse choice for viewing my website. I have also added a table to show how many animals that were reported harvested for the year 2018. I have also included border boxes with radial gradient sea green colors throughout my pages to break apart or separate topics.


V3 update

At present, I have continued to add more to my website.
I have added a comment or suggestion box to my website on my about page.
This enables users to comment or suggest changes to the website or what they would like to see more of. Included in my update I added 4 different videos. 1 video is on the home page while the other 3 are on the types of hunting page. I also added animation to my footer navigation links.
I hope you enjoy my website just as much as I did creating it.
