In Washington State there are many activities to do for hunting. Although it is somewhat hard to find areas to hunt in Western Washington, all it takes is to know where to look. Depending in the area that you are located in (area search) you can hunt certain species of animals, given you have all the required materials and documentation. Required Regulations

You can hunt for small game or big game in WA State.
Small game consist of small animals such as: rabbits, pheasants, geese or ducks. While big game consist of larger animals such as: deer, elk, black bear, cougar or mountain goat.
